# Governance

Career-Ops Capture uses a lightweight benevolent-dictator model, consistent with
the other repositories maintained by Rubicon TechVentures.

## Roles

- **Maintainer.** Dax Davis is the maintainer and final decision maker. The
  maintainer sets direction, reviews and merges pull requests, cuts releases, and
  is responsible for the security and privacy posture of the extension.
- **Contributors.** Anyone who opens an issue or pull request. Contributions are
  welcome under the terms in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## How decisions are made

Most decisions are made in the open on issues and pull requests. The maintainer
aims for consensus where practical, and makes the final call where consensus is
not reached. Changes that affect the privacy model, the permissions the extension
requests, or the set of hosts it can reach receive the highest scrutiny and are
documented in [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) and the changelog.

## Changing this model

If the project grows a regular contributor base, the maintainer may add
committers and formalize this document. Until then it stays intentionally small.
